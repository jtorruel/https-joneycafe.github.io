About Us




Joney Cafe is a cafeteria with a Chilean orientation. It is a family project with the objective of providing a homely space with homemade food typical of Chilean cuisine. This little piece of Chile was established by a Chilean woman who misses her country and combines her passion for cooking to bring a different place to the residents of Brookfield, Wisconsin.
